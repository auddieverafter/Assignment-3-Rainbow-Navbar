The Color Yellow

Yellow is the third color of the rainbow. It is a bright color that The color yellow universally represents the sun, joy, and optimism. Known as the brightest color the human eye notices, it grabs attention instantly, making it a natural choice for safety warnings, caution signs, and high-visibility traffic features.

My Favorite Shade of Yellow

My favorite shade of yellow is buttermilk yellow. Buttermilk yellow is a very light colored yellow that almost has a hue of orange in it. I like it because it's very mellow and soft.
