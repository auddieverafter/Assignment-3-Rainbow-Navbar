The Color Red

Red is the first color of the rainbow. It is a warm color that represents passion and energy. It is also a very romantic color used for holidays like Valentine's Day and Christmas. Red is visible in the spectrum (625–750 nanometers).

My favorite shade of red

My favorite shade of red is either raspberry red or strawberry red. Both are darker shades of red, but represent the exact colors of the berries.
