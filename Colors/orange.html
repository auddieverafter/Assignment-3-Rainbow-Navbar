The Color Orange

Orange is the second color of the rainbow. Orange symbolizes energy, enthusiasm, creativity, and warmth. Blending the passion of red with the joy of yellow, it also represents optimism, youthfulness, and social connection.

My favorite shade of Orange

My favorite shade of orange is aesthetic orange because I like very pastel colors, and aesthetic orange is a perfect representation of that.
