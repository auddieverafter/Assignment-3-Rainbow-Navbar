The Color Blue

Blue is the fifth color of the rainbow. Blue represents calmness, trust, stability, and wisdom. Because it mimics the open sky and ocean, it is widely associated with tranquility, intelligence, and reliability. However, it can also symbolize sadness, depression, or emotional coldness.

My Favorite Shade of Blue

My favorite shade of blue is baby blue. Blue is actually my favorite color and I love almost all shades of blue. I love baby blue the most because it's the most calming and soft color of all (in my opinion).
