The Color Purple

Purple is the fifth and last color of the rainbow. Purple represents royalty, wisdom, luxury, and creativity. It bridges the calm stability of blue and the fierce energy of red. Because purple dye was historically rare and expensive, it has long been associated with wealth, nobility, and power.

My Favorite Shade of Purple

My favorite shade of purple is thistle purple. I like thistle purple because it's similar to the flower color and it's more toned down than the vibrant purple everyone knows.
