The Color Green

Green is the fourth color of the rainbow. Green represents nature, vitality, and growth. Across various cultures and contexts, it is the primary color for renewal, fertility, and harmony. Because of its soothing presence in the natural world, green frequently represents health, peace, and balance.

My Favorite Shade of Green

My favorite shade of green is buttermilk yellow.
